Intro

Army brat turned Augusta native, Ari Joffrion created and launched Premier Barber Lounge in February 2019. Premier Barber Lounge is a business dedicated to ALL hair and personal care. Their goal is to provide customers with the opportunity to have multiple grooming needs met in one location with Great Cuts, Great Atmosphere, and Great Service.
